Introduction

Katkargarh Waterfall, located in the Kaimur district of Bihar, is a beautiful natural attraction surrounded by lush greenery. It is a popular spot for nature lovers and tourists seeking a peaceful escape.

History

The waterfall has been a natural treasure for centuries, known for its serene environment and picturesque landscape. The area around the waterfall is rich in flora and fauna, making it a perfect destination for nature enthusiasts.

How to Reach

Katkargarh Waterfall can be reached by road from nearby towns. The nearest railway station is in Sasaram, and the closest airport is in Varanasi, which is approximately 140 km away.

Opening Hours

The waterfall is accessible to visitors from sunrise to sunset, making it an ideal spot for a day trip.

Additional Information

The best time to visit Katkargarh Waterfall is during the monsoon season when the waterfall is in full flow. The surrounding area also offers opportunities for trekking and exploring the natural beauty of the Kaimur hills.
