Introduction

Piot Baba is a revered place of worship in Patna, known for its spiritual significance and the peaceful environment it offers. It attracts many visitors seeking tranquility and spiritual growth.

History

Piot Baba has been a significant spiritual site for decades, with a rich history of devotion and reverence. It is believed to be a place where devotees come to seek blessings and inner peace.

How to Reach

Piot Baba is located in Patna, accessible via local transportation such as auto-rickshaws and taxis. The nearest major landmarks include Patna Junction railway station and Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport, both of which are well-connected to the site.

Opening Hours

The site is open to visitors throughout the day. It is recommended to visit during early morning or evening to fully experience the serene atmosphere.

Additional Information

Visitors to Piot Baba can experience a range of spiritual activities and participate in local rituals. The site offers a serene environment conducive to meditation and reflection.
