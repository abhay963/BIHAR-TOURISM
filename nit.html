Introduction

NIT Ghat, located in Patna, is a popular spot along the banks of the Ganges River. It is a serene place, known for its peaceful environment and beautiful views of the river, making it a favorite destination for locals and visitors alike.

History

NIT Ghat is closely associated with the National Institute of Technology, Patna. It has been a place of cultural and spiritual significance, hosting various events and rituals over the years. The ghat has also been a site for river-based activities, including boat rides and festivals.

How to Reach

NIT Ghat is located in the heart of Patna, making it easily accessible by road. The nearest airport is Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport, and the nearest railway station is Patna Junction. Local transportation, such as auto-rickshaws and taxis, is readily available to reach the ghat.

Opening Hours

NIT Ghat is open to visitors all day, with the best time to visit being early morning or late evening to enjoy the sunrise or sunset over the Ganges River.

Additional Information

NIT Ghat is not just a place for relaxation but also a venue for spiritual gatherings and community events. It offers a peaceful environment for those looking to meditate or enjoy the natural beauty of the Ganges River.
