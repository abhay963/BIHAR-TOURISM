Introduction

Mahavir Mandir is one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Hanuman, located in Patna, Bihar. It is a significant pilgrimage site and attracts millions of devotees every year.

History

The temple has a long and storied history, with its origins believed to date back to ancient times. The current structure was renovated and expanded during the 20th century, making it one of the largest temples dedicated to Lord Hanuman in India.

How to Reach

Mahavir Mandir is situated near Patna Junction, making it easily accessible by road and rail. The nearest airport is Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport in Patna. The temple is located in the heart of the city and is well-connected by local transport.

Opening Hours

The temple is open to devotees from early morning until late evening. The general visiting hours are from 5:00 AM to 10:30 PM. Special aartis and rituals are conducted at specific times during the day.

Additional Information

The temple is particularly famous for its "Prasad" and "Naivedyam," which are special sweets offered to Lord Hanuman and later distributed among devotees. The temple also runs several charitable activities and a cancer hospital in Patna.
