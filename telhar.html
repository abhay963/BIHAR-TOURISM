Introduction

Telhar Waterfall, located in the Kaimur district of Bihar, is one of the most scenic and lesser-known waterfalls in the region. Surrounded by lush greenery and rocky terrain, it offers a breathtaking view and a tranquil environment.

History

Telhar Waterfall has been a hidden gem for many years. Although it hasn't been extensively commercialized, it has started gaining popularity among nature lovers and adventurers looking for a peaceful spot away from the hustle and bustle.

How to Reach

The waterfall is accessible via road from the nearest towns and cities. It is recommended to hire a vehicle or use public transportation to reach the location. The nearest major town is Bhabua, which is well connected to other parts of Bihar.

Opening Hours

Telhar Waterfall is open to visitors throughout the day. However, it is advisable to visit during daylight hours to enjoy the natural beauty safely.

Additional Information

Telhar Waterfall is best visited during the monsoon season when the water flow is at its peak, providing a spectacular view. The surrounding area is ideal for hiking and picnicking, making it a perfect spot for a day out with family and friends.
